GOVERNANCE ON THE INTERNET COMPUTER

Neurons

The governance of the Internet computer is controlled by the Network Nervous System. A neuron confers voting rights and other benefits.

The Internet Computer is a decentralized platform that aims to provide an open and free internet for all. As of May 10, 2021, the Blockchain is public and provides a decentralized internet at web speed. To prevent abuse or deliberate harm to the platform and community, blockchain platforms usually rely on governance. Voting rights are often attached to conditions that make it unattractive to misbehave while providing stability.
Dfinity's Internet Computer has developed a similar concept.
